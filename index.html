Data Deletion Instructions for Blast Buster

To request the deletion of your personal data associated with Blast Buster, please follow these steps:

Email Request:

  • Send an email to our support team at yehor.balinskyii@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request for Blast Buster."
  • In the email, please include your user ID or any other identifier used in the game, and explicitly state your request for data deletion.

Verification:

After receiving your request, our team may contact you for verification purposes. This is to ensure the security and rightful ownership of the data to be deleted.

Data Deletion from Third-Party Services:

  • Firebase Analytics & Messaging: Along with your email request, mention the need to delete data associated with Firebase. We will handle this process on your behalf.
  • Unity Ads: Visit the Unity Privacy Policy page for instructions on submitting a data deletion request.
  • Facebook SDK: You may need to manually remove app permissions from your Facebook account settings.
  • AppsFlyer SDK: Contact AppsFlyer support or follow the instructions on their privacy policy page for data deletion requests.

Confirmation of Deletion:

Once your data deletion request has been processed, you will receive a confirmation email from us.

Additional Assistance:

If you encounter any issues or have questions, please do not hesitate to contact our customer support team at yehor.balinskyii@gmail.com.

Please note that the deletion of your data is irreversible. Make sure you want to proceed with this action before submitting your request.
